Trainer Cards
* Boss’s Orders (Ghetsis) (Supporter)
   * Effect: Switch in 1 of your opponent’s Benched Pokémon to the Active Spot.
* Buddy-Buddy Poffin (Item)
   * Effect: Search your deck for up to 2 Basic Pokémon with 70 HP or less and put them onto your Bench.
* Hilda (Supporter)
   * Effect: Search your deck for an Evolution Pokémon and an Energy card, reveal them, and put them into your hand.
Judge (Supporter)
* Effect: Each player shuffles their hand into their deck and draws 4 cards.
Lillie’s Determination (Supporter)
* Effect: Shuffle your hand into your deck. Then, draw 6 cards. If you have exactly 0 knockouts done to your opponents, draw 8 cards instead.
Night Stretcher (Item)
* Effect: Put a Pokémon or a Basic Energy card from your discard pile into your hand.
Poké Pad (Item)
* Effect: Search your deck for a Pokémon that doesn't have a Rule Box, reveal it, and put it into your hand. Then, shuffle your deck. (Pokémon ex, Pokémon V, etc. have Rule Boxes.)
Pokégear 3.0 (Item)
* Effect: Look at the top 7 cards of your deck. You may reveal a Supporter card you find there and put it into your hand. Shuffle the other cards back into your deck.
Risky Ruins (Stadium)
* Effect: Whenever any player puts a Basic non-Darkness Pokémon onto their Bench during their turn, place 2 damage counters on that Pokémon.
Ultra Ball (Item)
* Effect: You can use this card only if you discard 2 other cards from your hand. Search your deck for a Pokémon, reveal it, and put it into your hand. Then, shuffle your deck.
Wally’s Compassion (Supporter)
* Effect: Heal all damage from 1 of your Mega Evolution Pokémon ex. If you healed any damage in this way, put all Energy attached to that Pokémon into your hand.


Energy Cards
* Ignition Energy (Special Energy)
   * Effect: Provides 1 Colorless Energy. If attached to an Evolution Pokémon, it provides 3 Colorless Energies instead. Discard it at the end of your turn.
* Basic Water Energy
* Basic Darkness Energy
Legacy Energy (ACE SPEC)
* Effect: As long as this card is attached to a Pokémon, it provides every type of Energy but provides only 1 Energy at a time.
* Special Effect: If the Pokémon this card is attached to is Knocked Out by damage from an attack from your opponent’s Pokémon, that player takes 1 knockout reward. This effect of your Legacy Energy can’t be applied more than once per game.
